About the Author

U.S. Oleagbor

U.S. Oleagbor — Udochukwu Samuel Oleagbor — is a Christian author and songwriter based in Lagos, Nigeria. His writing sits at the meeting point of careful Bible study and everyday life — convinced that God still speaks, and that most of what He says can be tested against what He has already written.

When he isn’t writing, he serves as a singer in his church choir and builds software tools for ministry and study. His books are written in loving memory of his parents.
